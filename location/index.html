Location

The Heart of Ponthir

 

 

 

 

 

 

Seren Fach is set in the heart of the village of Ponthir in an idyllic location adjacent to the local primary school, village cricket pitch and playing fields with breath taking views towards Twmbarlwm and the Welsh Mountains. We are conveniently situated close to the M4 motorway allowing easy access to Newport, Cwmbran, Pontypool, Cardiff and Bristol.
